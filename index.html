Features

Learn how to draw from scratch! View weekly drawing videos and feel more comfortable drawing in no time. Each month will have a theme with new videos available every week. Videos are available for one week, but you can sign up for the premium or ultimate packages to be able to watch ALL of our videos on demand!

Receive a monthly craft kit to explore new products and colors you can use with our weekly videos OR to experiment on your own. Each box will contain a variety of art supplies - you choose how to use them! The box will also include a handy tip guide for using the supplies and where to find more of the ones you fall in love with!

In addition to the tips provided in the weekly drawing videos, our craft kit come with a tip guide so you can constantly be improving your art. Remember there is no one right or wrong way to do art - so your only limit is your own imagination. Use this tip guide as a starting point to explore new media, but then see what else you can create with it!

About Us & What We Do

The Purple Paintbrush is a subscription service dedicated to nuturing and encouraging the arts. We believe everyone is an artist, it's just a matter of recognizing your abilities. We offer a variety of monthly options so you can decide which is right for you. Each month you can expect quality content - in the videos we produce AND in the craft kits full of art supplies that you'll receive. Just remember that with art there is no right or wrong. Join us and get your creative juices flowing again!
